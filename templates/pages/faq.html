Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I buy a game on BBGame?

Open the game page, click Add to cart, then complete checkout from your cart.

2. Which payment methods are available?

Payments are currently processed in demo mode. Additional payment providers can be enabled later.

3. Can I request a refund?

Yes, refund requests are handled manually by support after order verification.

4. How does delivery work for digital games?

Games are delivered digitally to your account right after successful order processing.

5. Do I need an account to place an order?

Yes. You need to register and log in to use cart, favorites, reviews, and order history.

6. How are discounts applied?

If a game has a discount, the final price is recalculated automatically and shown in the catalog and product page.
